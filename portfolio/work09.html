galdrastrákur (prototype)

project in prograss. contact me for more info.

made with unity and aseprite

Video was recorded in Unity game preview; consequently, lighting and rendering quality appears different in the playable version of the experience.

galdrastrákur is a "miniature" horror game where player must solve puzzles during the day so that they can escape “the witch” at night

Based on Icelandic Folk Song “Bium bium bambalo,” it's setting and style is based on Icelandic culture and magic.

Level Design

Programmatically, this game is currently assembled through ASCII level generation and tilemapping.

Currently, working on fixing colliders, developing shaders and user interface, and expanding levels. Also the addition of 3d models for the horror element.

I made the 2D models and their animations in aseprite, and all settings were assembled in Unity and coded with Jetbrains Rider.
