galdrastrákur is a "miniature" cursed game where the player must solve puzzles during the day so that they can escape “the witch” at night

Based on the Icelandic Folk Song “Bium bium bambalo,” it's setting and style is based on Icelandic culture and magic.

Level Design

Programmatically, this game is currently assembled through ASCII level generation and timeline sequences.

Currently, working on user testing and balancing issues.

Level Design

There are two endings right now: one where all the butter is collected, another where it's not.
