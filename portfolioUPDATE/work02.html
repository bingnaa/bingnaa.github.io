scattered love

coding project made in p5.js web editor, implementing handfrees.js

Needs use of camera. Takes a while to load and doesn't always detect hand gestures. Can lag depending on screen size.

Code that explores personal grief. played with the concept of grasping a "scattering" memory:

the harder you try to remember it, the faster it fades.

Project was coded in p5.js; it uses the handfree.js library. Detects when a person does a "hand-pinch" to transition between images.

These were the initial images used. Wanted it to have a feel of "looking into another world." Problem was that they were too detailed and didn't show up as well as I would like. The glitch effect also looked like a mistake, rather than intentional.

I decided to go in a more personal direction with the images that I made- I decided to focus on the loss of my rabbit. It also worked well with the concept that I wanted to go with: fleeting memories. Problem was that these images were also too detailed and didn't show up well when they were pixelated.

The final images were the ones that I used in project. They were modified with p5 pixel.

This final design came about through multiple iterations. Needed something that would translate well with the code that I had written. More abstract than detailed. A monochrome palette also better portrayed the feeling of grief that I wanted to present in the work.
